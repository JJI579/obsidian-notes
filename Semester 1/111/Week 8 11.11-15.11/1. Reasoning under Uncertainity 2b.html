#conditional-independence

suggest that we have to events, A and B normal independence means the result of A will not be determined from the result of B, and vice versa

Conditional independence is that imagine there is a third event, C and this has happened it means that knowing whether A happened will not implicate / provide further information on relation for B to happen but you may know information for A since C has initially happened
